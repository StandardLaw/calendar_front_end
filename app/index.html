About This Project

The Boston Civic Technology Calendar has been looooong in the works. It is a product of many like-minded folks in the Civic Technology ecosystem who want to foster more open collaboration and sharing. The platform itself is built by the Code For Boston community. The events you'll see here are sourced from many groups in the area.
